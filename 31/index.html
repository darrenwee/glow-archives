Please note a small change in the programme.

The 31st GLOW Colloquium will be hosted by the Centre for Research in Linguistics and Language Sciences, Newcastle University, from 26th to 28th March 2008, with additional workshops running on 25th and 29th.

The review process is now finished and all authors should have been in formed of decisions. If you have not heard anything please contact glow31@ncl.ac.uk with the full title of your paper and all co-authors. Competition was tough and only 17% of colloquium abstracts were accepted! If you would like to see the reviewers' comments, please follow the instructions on the reviewing page.

The provisional programme can now be viewed here.

Registration is now possible and accommodation can now be booked via the appropriate link.

Invited speakers: Luigi Rizzi (Siena), Arto Anttila (Stanford)

The workshops are as follows:
Tuesday, March 25 (30-minute papers)

  • Categorical phonology and gradient facts
  • Evidentiality
  • DP types and feature syntax

Saturday, March 29 (45-minute papers)

  • Language Contact
  • Principles of Linearization
