Reviewing

Reviewing for GLOW is now over. Thanks very much to all those who contributed!

Authors can now view all comments from reviewers by accessing their Pasha account. If you know your password, go to:

http://www.netskills.ac.uk/projects/glow2008/pasha/author/

then enter your username (the email address you used to register), and your password, when prompted. If no comments appear, it is because reviewers, unfortunately, did not give any.

If you cannot remember your password, go to:

http://www.netskills.ac.uk/projects/glow2008/pasha/register/forgot_password.cgi?am=author

You can then retrieve your password by entering the email address you used when you registered. Please try all email addresses/forms before contacting us!

If you have any technical problems please contact enquiries@netskills.ac.uk
