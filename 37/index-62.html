Hello world!

Welcome on the website for the 37th annual meeting of Generative Linguistics in the Old World (GLOW).

GLOW 37 will consist of three events: the main colloquium, two workshops and a Spring School. It will take place in Brussels from April 2 until April 11, 2014.

On this website you will find information concerning accommodation, workshops, the venue, program, calls for papers etc. You can also stay up to date by following:

  • our Facebook page
  • our Twitter account
  • our RSS feed.
