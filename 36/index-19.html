Social Events

There will be two social events organised by the local GLOW organisers. Other social events of a more spontaneous nature will have to be organised by the GLOW particpants themselves, but feel free to ask questions about what's possible to do at nights in the city of Lund, etc.

 

RECEPTION    Tuesday 2 April, 5:30-8:00

In connection to the GLOW venue

Included in the conference fee

 

CONFERENCE DINNER   Wednesday 3 April, at 7:30

Grand Hotel, address: Bantorget 1

  • Grand Hotel on a Lund map

Most people paid for this dinner when they registered for the conference.
